Our Story

Our story started with an idea: To provide a space for friends to hangout while consuming a dish. We are commited to provide quality customer service while also sustaining a safe environment for customers to hangout over a burger. Our organization is spread across all 50 states, and we are putting forth an effort to expand into territories outside the United States. Our next target for expansion is the peaceful country of Japan.
